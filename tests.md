# Tests

## Test 1: Push range and duration limits (trialrun6a-6j)

10 consecutive training runs fired from phone while walking. Tested whether
remote control holds beyond 20 minutes and 1 mile. Result: 40+ minutes,
2+ miles out, all 10 runs completed and pushed to GitHub.

## Test 2: Deliberate phone/internet disruption (trialrun7a-7b)

2 training runs fired from phone while deliberately breaking mobile internet
and the Claude app. Result: training completed on both. Once launched, the
phone doesn't matter.

## Test 3: Deliberate laptop-side disconnection (trialrun8)

Walked back to the laptop, fired a run from phone, then deliberately
disconnected remote control from the laptop side. Result: training completed.
Once launched, even the Claude Code session doesn't matter.
